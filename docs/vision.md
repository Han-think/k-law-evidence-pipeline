# Vision

K-Law Evidence Pipeline exists to reduce the first barrier of legal action: organizing the case.

Many people do not lose because they have no grievance. They lose time, confidence, and leverage because their facts, dates, documents, screenshots, messages, and legal sources are scattered.

This project aims to provide a practical middle layer:

```text
ordinary person
→ structured facts and evidence
→ official-source legal references
→ draft documents
→ better professional review or public filing preparation
```

## What this project is

- A local-first case organization tool
- An evidence timeline builder
- A citation-aware legal source retrieval layer
- A draft document assistant
- A reusable pipeline for civic legal preparation

## What this project is not

- A lawyer
- A court filing automation service
- A final legal opinion generator
- A system that guarantees compensation or victory

## Social goal

The project is free and open because legal preparation should not be limited only to people who can immediately afford professional help.

Better organization cannot solve every legal problem, but it can make help more accessible, reduce confusion, and improve the quality of conversations with lawyers, agencies, courts, and public aid institutions.
