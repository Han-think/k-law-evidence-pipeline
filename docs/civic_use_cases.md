# Civic Use Cases

This project is designed for people who need to prepare better before seeking help.

## Ordinary citizens

- organize a privacy leak issue
- prepare a consumer complaint
- structure a telecom service dispute
- build a timeline for a small civil claim
- prepare materials before public legal aid consultation

## Small businesses and founders

- check relevant law candidates before launching a service
- organize contract dispute materials
- prepare a demand letter draft
- review compliance source candidates
- separate business facts from assumptions

## Public officials and civic workers

- organize citizen complaint records
- check official source candidates
- build neutral timelines
- prepare explanation materials
- reduce unclear source citation

## Researchers and educators

- study how official sources connect to real disputes
- create sample evidence packs
- teach source verification
- compare source reliability levels

## Community organizers

- collect repeated dispute patterns
- structure group complaint materials
- prepare public issue summaries
- separate verified facts from public opinion

## Boundary

The project helps with preparation and organization. It does not replace professional legal advice, public institutions, or official filing review.
